ABOUT ALL INGREDIENTS

The overall nutritional value of a dish depends on portion sizes, preparation methods, and other factors. It's always a good idea to enjoy these foods in moderation as part of a balanced diet.

Flour:
Provides carbohydrates, fiber, and some essential nutrients like iron and B vitamins.

Eggs:
Excellent source of high-quality protein, vitamins (such as vitamin B12 and riboflavin), and minerals (such as selenium).

Butter:
Contains fat-soluble vitamins (like vitamin A) and provides a source of energy.

Sugar:
Provides sweetness and energy. However, it should be consumed in moderation.

Tomato sauce:
Contains lycopene, an antioxidant that may have potential health benefits, including reducing the risk of certain cancers.

Cheese:
Provides calcium, protein, and other essential nutrients like vitamin B12.

Vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, mushrooms, onions):
Rich in various vitamins, minerals, and dietary fiber, which contribute to a well-balanced diet.

Romaine lettuce:
Low in calories and packed with vitamins A, C, and K, as well as minerals like calcium and potassium.

Parmesan cheese:
Contains calcium, protein, and phosphorus, which are essential for bone health.

Garlic:
Known for its potential health benefits, including boosting the immune system and reducing the risk of certain diseases.

Lemon juice:
A good source of vitamin C and adds a tangy flavor to the salad.

Anchovy fillets (optional):
Rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which are beneficial for heart health.
